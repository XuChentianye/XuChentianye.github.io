04 : About me

Hello, I am Chentianye Xu (Chinese: 徐陈天野) , a student majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Wu Yuzhang Honors College / College of Computer Science, Sichuan University. I became an undergraduate student of College of Computer Science of Sichuan University in Sep 2019 after having passed the National College Entrance Examination.

After that, I was admitted to Wu Yuzhang Honors College of Sichuan University by passing the test of innovative abilities and scientific research potential of freshmen (about 1.5% of all freshmen were enrolled; the introduction of Wu Yuzhang Honors College can be viewed here). After getting admitted into Wu Yuzhang Honors College, besides studying major courses, I also received general education, practical education, interdisciplinary education, and academic research training.

Subsequently, after passing the academic ability test of College of Computer Science, I entered the newly opened Artificial Intelligence Experimental Class and started to major in Artificial Intelligence (36 students, which accounted for about 8% of all freshmen in College of Computer Science, were enrolled).

I am expected to graduate in June 2023 with a bachelor’s degree.
