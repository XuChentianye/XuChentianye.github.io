04 : About me (before Sep 2019)

Hello, I am Chentianye Xu (Chinese: 徐陈天野), a student majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Wu Yuzhang Honors College / College of Computer Science, Sichuan University. Before I got admitted into Sichuan University, I lived in Nantong, Jiangsu and studied there for 19 years. In 2016, I was admitted into the Experimental Class of Jiangsu Bencha Senior High School with the first place in the school. I served as Head of Study Department of Student Union when I was in this school.
